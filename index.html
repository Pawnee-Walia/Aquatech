Welcome To Aquatech

We introduce ourselves as an organization specializing in design, supply, execution of fountains, swimming pools, water features & pop- up sprinkler systems. Our strength lies in executing turnkey projects involving design, civil construction, providing & fixing all mechanical/ hydraulic equipments, electrical, plumbing, finishing works & commissioning the project to the complete satisfaction of the client. This results in client getting an advantage of turnkey solution to the entire work through single agency which in turn ensures reliable guarantee/ warranty from a single vendor, project completion within stipulated time frame (avoids multiple working by various agencies) & competitive pricing resulting in total customer satisfaction. Providing after commissioning service for water features ensures our fountains keep functioning for years!

About Us

In house team of qualified & professional engineers (with combined experience of more than 16 years in civil construction/engineering and eight years in swimming pools/ water features in India & middle east countries) helps us to operate efficiently and execute projects successfully with the best of quality and within stipulated time schedules.
